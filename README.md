**What**  
A complete KoboToolbox workflow: XLSForm, simulated dataset, simple analysis and visualizations for a household WASH (Water, Sanitation & Hygiene) survey.

**Objective & Indicators**
- Objective: Measure household WASH access in a small study area to identify gaps.
- Key indicators to show in portfolio:
  - % households with improved water source
  - % households with improved sanitation
  - % households with soap at handwashing facility
  - Distribution of primary water source types


**Project summary**

Design, deploy and analyze a KoboToolbox household survey to measure household access to water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) services; produce cleaned dataset, indicator calculations, charts and a short report.
